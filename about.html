About Us

myGhanaLinkUp.com

Ghanalink.com has been created to facilitate online community connectedness and/or communication around shared interests. The website seeks to serve young professionals from the Ghanaian diaspora and will function like a community center, a one-stop site that will offer vital community information as well as networking opportunities for generations to come.

Concept

The site will become the centre of our social life and the social life of Ghanaians in particular and generally West Africans in the diaspora.

Community spaces play an important role in the success of a city and its inhabitants. They become the foundation to which those living and visiting in the area come together and communicate and develop relationships.

The village square or the Market Square as others call it, offers opportunities to embrace the history and culture of the area and bring the community together using this common bond.

Developing on the Community Center metaphor, the site will focus initially on embracing the history and culture of the people of Ghana and those in the diaspora.

Selected Areas of Focus

1. Provision of facilities for people to create their professional resumes and professional interest groups and following.

2. Mentoring program for young professionals

3. Dating functionality where confidential algorithms will pair and promote relationships.

4. Community Profiles and Bulleting Boards. These will host various interest groups, ideas and postings relevant to our use community e.g.

• social meet ups
• health information sessions
• cultural events (i.e. food, festivals, etc.)
• career networking events
• mentor programs
